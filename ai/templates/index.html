How Fraud Is Detected
Behavioral & Heuristics
  • Amount exceeds threshold: Adaptive threshold based on recent spending patterns per user.
  • High frequency anomaly: Abnormally high transaction rate within a time window.
  • High transaction velocity: Many transactions compressed in a short period.
  • Repeated micro-merchant pattern: Multiple small amounts to same merchant/IP.
  • Cumulative risk: Sum of recent amounts crosses risk threshold.
ML & Network Signals
  • Ensemble risk score: RandomForest + XGBoost probability (when trained).
  • Linked accounts: Shared merchants/IPs form suspicious clusters.
  • AI explanations: On-demand AI summary for selected transactions.
